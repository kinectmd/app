Therapy at Your Fingertips

KINECT offers a digital platform that provides 24/7 mental health services for physicians by the integration of telepsychiatry services, group support services, and meditation. Our mission is to create a safe and secure environment for frontline physicians to share their experiences, get emotional support from their peers and take better care of themselves.
